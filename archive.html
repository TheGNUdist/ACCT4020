Business Process Diagramming

Process diagrams are a useful tool for understanding the informational needs of the firm. This lecture will introduce multiple diagramming models and explain how to read and write diagrams. This topic has no readings, and we will not have student presentations next week. Instead of student presentations, on Tuesday, February 16, I will demonstrate a popular free online diagramming tool. After the lecture, I will add a link to a video of the demonstration.

The live students should bring their solution to class on Tuesday, February 16. For the second assignment: All students should upload a PDF, PNG, or SVG of their diagram to Dropbox by Saturday, February 20 @ 23.59.
