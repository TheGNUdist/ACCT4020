Practice

An important component of control is the removal of access following employee departure. Your will simulate this scenario now that the semester has ended by deleting your Codeanywhere account. Log in to your Codeanywhere account, and select Account Dashboard from the menu in the top-right corner of the screen. On the Account page, select Delete Account. Enter your account password. Before selecting Confirm, take a screenshot of the browser window. Then select Confirm to complete account deletion. Finally, upload your screenshot to Dropbox on eCourseware.
