Professionalism Assignment

Professionalism is only valuable to those who commit to live it. Once you have read the Professionalism lecture, write a one-page, single-spaced memo explaining your commitment to professionalism in this and your other courses, in your career, and throughout your life. Your memo should:

  1. Have a proper header (To:, From:, Date:, and Re:).
  2. Use proper spelling and grammar.
  3. Reference specific principles from the lecture and the required readings.
  4. Be at least 500 words.
  5. Not exceed one page.
