Background

The most common framework for writing metadata is eXtensible Markup Language (XML), so that is how I will draft this solution. The goal is to create unique pairs of attributes and values—no attribute may have multiple values, and no attribute may be used more than once—that link accounts to the dollar amount of the modification and whether it is a debit or a credit. This is one possible solution:

Solution

  • <DATE>20X51231</DATE>
  • <DEBIT1>Revenue</DEBIT1>

  • <CREDIT1AMOUNT>40000</CREDIT1AMOUNT>
