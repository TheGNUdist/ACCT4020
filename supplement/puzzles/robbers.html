Interrogation

The police rounded up Jim, Bud and Sam yesterday, because one of them was suspected of having robbed a bank. The three suspects made the following statements under intensive questioning:

  • Jim: I'm innocent.
  • Bud: I'm innocent.
  • Sam: Bud is guilty.

If exactly one of these statements is true, prove who robbed the bank. (Your proof must be irrefutable; it may not rely on your feeling that one testimony is “suspicious.”) Write your solution and proof, save it as PDF, and upload it to Dropbox on eCourseware.
