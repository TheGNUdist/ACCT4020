Metadata Assignment

XML is the most widely implemented metadata language, and this (longer) assignment will increase your familiarity with XML. First, visit http://www.w3schools.com/xml/xml_whatis.asp. This is the beginning of an XML tutorial. Complete all chapters under the left navigation heading XML Tutorial, except for XML Certificate. After completing the XML Quiz, print the results page of the quiz to PDF. Your grade for this assignment will be your quiz score. You may retake the quiz as many times as you wish, but you should only submit your best attempt. I will grade whichever attempt you submit.
