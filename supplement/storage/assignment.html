SQL Assignment

Structured Query Language (SQL) is the primary language for interacting with relational databases. This assignment is the first of two to increase your ability to work with this language for both storage and data analytics purposes. Complete the SQL Tutorial and SQL Quiz on W3Schools. After completing the SQL Quiz, print the results page of the quiz to PDF, and upload it to Dropbox on eCourseware. You should not save the results as HTML, nor should you take a screenshot of the browser window; you should print the results page to PDF. (If you have not learned how to print a website to PDF, let me Google that for you: IE, Firefox, Safari, Chrome.) Your grade for this assignment will be your quiz score. You may retake the quiz as many times as you wish, but you should only submit your best attempt.
