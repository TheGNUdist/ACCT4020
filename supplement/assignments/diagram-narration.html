Diagram Narration

This assignment will give you experience reading diagrams and writing narratives of the business processes. On the next screens, you will find several diagrams. Your task is to write a narrative for each diagram. Your narrative should explain which agent performs which task(s) in which order. (NB: Data flow does not always indicate control flow.) Some of the diagrams on the following screens are in the lecture, and you may read the narrative in the lecture after you have written your own in order to verify its accuracy and completeness. However, this assignment will not be useful to you in learning to read and narrate diagrams unless you write your narrative first and only look at the narrative in the lecture afterwards. Write your narratives in a single word processor file, export the file to PDF, and upload it to Dropbox on eCourseware.
