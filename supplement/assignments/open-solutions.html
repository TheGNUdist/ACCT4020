Open Solutions - Intro

The technology and business world rely on all sorts of open standards, open formats, and open source software. Many more individuals would likely use these open solutions to everyday computing needs, if they were aware of them. This assignment will give you practice in searching for open solutions. On the next screen are a list of demands. Each demand asks for an open source software application or an open format. For each demand, you should research, find, and recommend the best solution or solutions. Depending on the question, you may also need to identify the software license that the application uses. Create a document listing your response to each demand, export the document to PDF, and upload it to Dropbox on eCourseware. I will grade you on your accuracy in finding open solutions.

  1. I need an open source office suite, and it must use either the Mozilla Public License or the Apache License. What is your single best recommendation, and what license or licenses does this office suite use?
  2. I need an open source web browser, and it must be as similar as possible to Google Chrome. What is your single best recommendation, and what license or licenses does this web browser use?
  3. I need an open source graphics editor, and it must use the GNU General Public License version 3. What is your single best recommendation?
  4. I need an open source media player, and it must use either the GNU General Public License or the GNU General Public License version 2. What is your single best recommendation, and what license or licenses does this media player use?
  5. I need an open format for word processing documents, spreadsheets, and presentations. What is your single best recommendation?
  6. I need two open formats for audio compression, and neither can be MPEG-3! What are your two best recommendations?
  7. I need two open formats for video compression, and neither can be MPEG-4! What are your two best recommendations?
  8. I need two open source operating systems as alternatives to Microsoft Windows and Apple macOS. One must use the GNU General Public License version 2, and the other must use the BSD license. What are your two best recommendations?
