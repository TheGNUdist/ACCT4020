Flowchart Drawing

Being able to read flowcharts and diagrams enables you to understand when others communicate business processes to you, but being able to draw flowcharts and diagrams enables you to communicate business processes to others. This assignment will give you practice drawing a flowchart. The next screen displays a flowchart, and you should make a copy of it using the diagramming software of your choice. Draw.io is a free, online diagramming tool that you may use, but you may choose another software tool if you prefer. You can find helpful training videos on drawing flowcharts on the Draw.io website. If you use Draw.io, all required shapes will be in the Basic, Advanced, and Flowchart templates. When you have completed your flowchart, export the flowchart as a PDF file, and upload the file to Dropbox on eCourseware.
