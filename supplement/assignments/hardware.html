Interactive Assignment

Computer hardware enables you to do your job, but when hardware becomes outdated, it may no longer be able to meet the demands you place on it. For this assignment, you will research replacement hardware for your current computer. First, evaluate the hardware on your current computer or mobile device. List CPU model, word length, number of cores, clock speed, RAM capacity, and storage type and capacity. Second, select a replacement computer or mobile device and list its CPU model, word length, number of cores, clock speed, RAM capacity, and storage type and capacity. Write both lists into a word processor document, export the document to PDF, and upload the file to Dropbox on eCourseware.
