Classification

Data classification through the use of metadata is necessary in order to preserve data accuracy and security. This assignment will give you practice applying metadata to data using attribute-value pairs, XML, and XBRL. On each of the following screens, you will find unclassified data. Your task is to apply metadata to the data using the specified framework. Some of the examples on the following screens are in the lecture, and you may read the solution in the lecture after you have written your own in order to verify its accuracy and completeness. However, this assignment will not be useful to you in learning to apply metadata unless you draft your own solution first and only look at the solution in the lecture afterwards. Write your solutions in a single word processor file, export the file it to PDF, and upload it to Dropbox on eCourseware.
