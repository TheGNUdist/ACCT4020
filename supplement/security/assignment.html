Writing Assignment

Your assignment is to answer the following question: “What are the pros and cons of making population data of financial transactions available to investors, instead of the more traditional summary financial statements, and which report form (population data or summary reports) would you recommend?” Before answering this question, read Consequences of Big Data and Formalization on Accounting and Auditing Standards.
