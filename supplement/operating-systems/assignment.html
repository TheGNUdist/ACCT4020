Linux Assignment

In order to make it convenient for you to gain experience with a Linux terminal without requiring you to modify your current computer, I have selected an online service that hosts installations of Linux operating systems. Your assignment is to create a free account with Codeanywhere and add a new Blank Ubuntu 14.04 Container connection to that account. Once you have created the account and container, turn on the container, if necessary, open an SSH Terminal, and take a screenshot. Take some time to become acquainted with the shell, and then complete the Operating System quiz. The quiz will ask you for your Codeanywhere username and password, and it will also test your understanding of basic Linux commands.
