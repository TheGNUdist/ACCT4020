Linux Assignment

In order to make it convenient for you to gain experience with a Linux terminal without requiring you to modify your current computer, I have selected an online service that hosts installations of Linux operating systems. Your assignment is to create a free account with Codeanywhere and add a new Blank Ubuntu 14.04 Container connection to you account and name the container your first name and last name, for example I would name my container ProfessorCoyne. Once you have created the account and container, turn on the container, if necessary, open an SSH Terminal, and take a screenshot. Upload the screenshot to Dropbox on eCourseware. (If you have not learned how to take a screenshot, let me Google that for you: Windows, macOS, Chrome OS.)
