Writing Assignment

This course is likely very different from what you had expected. In the past, students have made it far into the semester thinking, “This seems more like an MIS course,” or, “I thought that this would be about accounting.” I try to dispel any misconceptions as early as possible by showing why accounting architecture is important to accountants. Similarly, when interviewing with a future employer, you should be able to communicate why what you have learned at school is important. For this assignment, write a one-page, single-spaced memo explaining what accounting architecture is and how it will make you a better employee and a stronger accountant. Your memo should have a proper header (i.e., To:, From:, Date:, and Subject:) and be at least 500 words. Save your memo as a PDF, and upload it to Dropbox on eCourseware.
