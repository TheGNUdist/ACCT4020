Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Q: I downloaded the 64-bit ISO, but on the “Name and operating system” screen, the Version drop-down says (32-bit). What should I do?
    A: Your computer has 64-bit virtualization disabled. You can either enable 64-bit virtualization in your firmware interface, or you can download the 32-bit ISO.
  2. Q: I downloaded the 64-bit ISO, but when I boot the virtual machine, I only get a black screen/error message and have no option to install the operating system. What should I do?
    A: Your computer has 64-bit virtualization disabled. You can either enable 64-bit virtualization in your firmware interface, or you can download the 32-bit ISO.
  3. Q: After completing the install, I reboot the virtual machine, but the screen is black. What should I do?
    A: In most cases, all you need to do is reset the virtual machine again, then it will boot properly.
